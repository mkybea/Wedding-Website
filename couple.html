The Couple

Katie and Matt met their Freshman year of College on the campus of UVA-Wise in beautiful Southwest Virginia. They became fast friends, and started dating in the fall of 2007.

Over the years, they enjoyed the ups and downs of college life: playing games on snow-days, group projects and interesting classes, meeting new people, and, sadly, a number of all-night study parties. But even through it all, they still remained ever supportive and true to each other.

By 2013, they were done with school: Matt having gotten a BS in Computer Science and Katie a BS in Environmental Science. They found themselves living together in a townhouse near their Alma Mater with their three baby-cats, Guen, Snow, and Linus. They each have enjoyed steady work and a happy home life.

By 2015, however, being so far away from family began to wear on them. They packed up their things and ventured north with their cats, closer to family. There they have settled into the small town of Culpeper.

After 9 wonderful years, Katie asked Matt to marry her. He said yes.
